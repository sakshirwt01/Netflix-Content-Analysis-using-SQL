🎬 **Netflix Content Analysis Using SQL**

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Netflix's content catalog using SQL. The objective was to explore trends in movies and TV shows, identify top-performing countries and directors, analyze content ratings, and generate meaningful business insights from the dataset.

The project demonstrates practical SQL skills including data cleaning, aggregation, filtering, sorting, grouping, and analytical querying.

🎯 Objectives

- Clean and prepare the dataset for analysis.
- Handle missing values in important columns.
- Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows.
- Identify top countries contributing content to Netflix.
- Discover the most common content ratings.
- Analyze content growth trends over the years.
- Generate actionable business insights from the data.

📂 Dataset Information

Dataset: Netflix Movies and TV Shows Dataset

The dataset contains information such as:

- Show ID
- Title
- Type (Movie/TV Show)
- Director
- Country
- Release Year
- Rating
- Duration
- Genre

🧹 Data Cleaning

Before performing analysis, the following data-cleaning steps were completed:

- Identified missing values in the Director column.
- Identified missing values in the Country column.
- Replaced NULL values with "Unknown".
- Verified that no missing values remained in the cleaned columns.

📊 Business Questions Solved

1. How many Movies and TV Shows are available on Netflix?

2. How many records contain missing Director information?

3. How many records contain missing Country information?

4. Which countries have the highest number of Netflix titles?

5. What are the most common content ratings on Netflix?

6. Who are the top 10 directors with the most Netflix titles?

7. Which movies were released after 2020?

8. What are the longest movies available on Netflix?

9. How has Netflix content grown over the years?

10. What percentage of Netflix content consists of Movies and TV Shows?

11. How can countries be ranked based on the number of Netflix titles?

🔍 Key Findings

- Movies represent the majority of Netflix's content library.
- The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
- TV-MA is among the most common content ratings.
- Netflix content has experienced significant growth over time.
- A small number of directors contribute a large share of total content.
- The platform offers content from a wide range of countries and regions.
